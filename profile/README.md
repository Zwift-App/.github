## Setup Snags and Practical Fixes

Pairing drops: restart Bluetooth, move competing devices away, then reconnect the zwift trainer inside the zwift app.  
Power feels wrong: recalibrate the zwift kickr, zwift kickr core, or other smart trainer and repeat a steady test ride.  
Shifting is inconsistent: check zwift click battery status, zwift cog alignment, and firmware updates.  
Training zones feel too hard: repeat a zwift ftp test after recovery and compare recent zwift power data.  
Account confusion: use my zwift to verify subscriptions, ride history, connected devices, and profile details.
